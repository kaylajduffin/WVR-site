The Problem

Generosity shouldn't be this hard.

Billions in donor capital sits idle or is allocated inefficiently — not because generosity is absent, but because the infrastructure connecting donors to organizations is broken.

For Donors

  • ✕ You give to the same 1–2 organizations by default — not because you've chosen them, but because you don't know where else to look.
  • ✕ Trust is the #1 barrier. You can't quickly verify if a new nonprofit is legitimate, effective, or aligned with your values.
  • ✕ You donate and never hear what your money actually accomplished. Impact is invisible.

For Nonprofits

  • ✕ Staff spend 20–30% of their time on cold prospecting and grant applications instead of serving communities.
  • ✕ Grant cycles take 6–18 months, creating cash-flow uncertainty and forcing programs to pause.
  • ✕ Cold outreach yields low conversion rates. You need donors who are already interested — not skeptical strangers.

The Hope Score

Impact measured by the people who experience it.

Most platforms measure nonprofits by financial ratios or self-reported data. WVR measures what actually matters: outcomes reported by the communities being served.

  • Beneficiary-reported: Surveys go directly to the people nonprofits serve.
  • Cross-comparable: Compare a food bank to a literacy program on a common scale.
  • Real-time: Scores update as new data arrives — a living metric, not an annual snapshot.
